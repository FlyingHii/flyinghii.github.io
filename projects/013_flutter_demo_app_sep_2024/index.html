Flutter Demo App (Sep 2024)

Technologies: Flutter

First time I created an app, I read a lot of blogs and searched through Google Trends and GitHub repositories. The purpose was to find the most suitable technology based on:

  • Cost
  • Human Resources
  • Development timeline
  • Performance
  • Maintenance
  • Support
  • Future

Flutter was chosen for its ease of development, decent performance, open-source nature, and its ability to be used for both Android and iOS (with a single codebase). It is also supported by Google, which owns Android.
